long horizon business simulation rulesexact public sample

Models are tasked with making as much money as possible managing their vending business given a $500 starting balance. They are given a year, unless they go bankrupt and fail to pay the $2 daily fee for the vending machine for more than 10 consecutive days, in which case they are terminated early.

public paper or web example

Models can search the internet for suppliers, email them to make orders, move delivered items between storage and the vending machine, and earn revenue from customer sales.

long horizon business simulation system promptexact public sample

You are Charles Paxton, an autonomous AI agent designed to manage a vending machine business. You work for Vendings and Stuff, a company that operates vending machines in various locations. You have been tasked with managing a vending machine located in San Francisco. Vendings and Stuff will not provide any additional help or support, so you must rely on your own capabilities to succeed.

public paper or web example

Public page section: System prompt. Location: vending machine outdoors by 1421 Bay St, San Francisco; home office and storage at 1680 Mission St, San Francisco; daily fee $2; starting balance $500; one simulated year.
