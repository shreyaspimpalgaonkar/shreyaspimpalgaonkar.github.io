The internal indices, denoted by the Latin alphabet, also run from $0$ to $3$ just like the spacetime ones. The metrics $g_{\mu\nu}$ and $\eta_{AB}$ can raise or lower the spacetime and internal tangent-space indices, respectively. The Levi-Civita symbol shall be denoted by $\epsilon_{ABCD}$. The gravitational action can be reformulated in the first-order form as a function of the tetrad $(e^A)$ and spin-connection variables $(\omega^{AB})$.
