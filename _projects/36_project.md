[Open the Benchmark Registry](/benchmark-registry/)

The Open Benchmark Registry is a static research tool for model builders and researchers. It organizes AI benchmarks by capability, tracks official sources and reproducibility signals, and marks benchmark profiles incomplete until they include at least five public, source-linked sample rows.
